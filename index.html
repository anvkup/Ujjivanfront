AGRI LOANS

Agri Group Loan

Helping Farmers realise their dreams

Ujjivan SFB’s vision to serve the needy and deserving extends to every corner of the country. We aim to serve our rural population who can benefit from our offerings for all their agricultural requirements. Also, we understand the world of our Indian farmer who is prepared to grow through a modernised and progressive outlook and farming methods.

Our products are designed to add convenience and comfort to the lives of farmers. Our Agri Group Loans suit their needs and help them achieve their dreams. These are need-based loans given at moderate rates under the Joint Liability Group (JLG) model of our parent micro-lending institution.

These are purpose-based loans given at moderate rates under the Joint Liability Group (JLG) model of our parent micro-lending institution.

Agriculture & Allied Activities covered under Ujjivan SFB Agri Group Loan

  • Livestock maintenance/ purchase
  • Sericulture
  • Tractor Maintenance / Equipment Purchase

Features

  • Interest Rate 15% pa
  • Minimum Age 21 Years
  • Maximum Age 60 Years
  • Processing Fee 3% of the Loan Amount
  • Loan Amount Up to ₹5 Lakhs
  • Loan Tenure 12-48 Months

Loan Amount

  • Minimum Rs. 50,000 & Maximum Rs. ₹5 Lakhs

Tenure

  • 12-48 Months

Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: 3% + GST

Kisan Suvidha Loan

Something special, for those who are special to us

At Ujjivan SFB, we provide loans to suit a farmer’s many requirements, ranging from farming improvements to other allied activities. Our loans support these different activities, paving the way for farmers to earn an income.

These are collateral-free loans for which we have made the repaying options easy and flexible.

  • Sericulture
  • Goat Rearing

Kisan Pragati Card

Loan for a Progressive Farmer

We would like to join hands with farmers and help them achieve progress and gain success. Kisan Pragati Card is a specially designed loan product with a specific focus on small and marginal farmers.

Through this loan, we aim to provide adequate timely credit to farmers for needs such as working capital for crop production, term loan for farm maintenance, farm development, consumption needs of farmer households allied agriculture activities and other investment needs, including unexpected farm exigencies.

We have also customized the product to suit the needs of modern and progressive farmers, who can benefit from this unique offering in all seasons, throughout the year.

Features

  • Interest Rate 15% pa
  • Minimum Age 21 Years
  • Maximum Age 60 Years
  • Processing Fee 3% of the Loan Amount
  • Loan Amount Up to ₹5 Lakhs
  • Loan Tenure 12-48 Months

Loan Amount

  • Minimum Rs. 50,000 & Maximum Rs. 10,00,000

Tenure

  • Upto 5 Years
